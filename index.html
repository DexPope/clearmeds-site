The Problem

The FDA Crackdown Is Already Here

If your clinic depends on a compounding pharmacy for peptides, you're exposed. The FDA has escalated enforcement against 503A and 503B compounding pharmacies — and the disruptions are accelerating.

Warning letters. Cease-and-desist orders. Facility shutdowns — sometimes with less than a week's notice. Clinics that built their peptide programs around compounders are discovering the hard way that their supply chain was never as stable as they thought.

Patients mid-protocol are left without medication. Staff scramble to find alternatives. Revenue from your highest-margin services evaporates overnight. And the compounders that are still operating? Many are tightening their product lines, raising prices, or quietly cutting corners to stay afloat.

Proprietary Technology

The Inclusion Complex

Traditional peptides are fragile — lyophilized powder, reconstitution requirements, cold chain, rapid degradation. Our proprietary inclusion complex changes everything.

By encapsulating peptide molecules in a cyclodextrin-based matrix, we deliver vials that arrive pre-reconstituted, molecularly stable, and ready to administer — no mixing, no cold chain, no guesswork.
